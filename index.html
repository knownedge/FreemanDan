About Freemandan

FreemanDan is a brand designer, creative artist, psychedelic explorer, mystic and game designer.

All around good guy, he is kind, compassionate, loyal and virtuous. Obsessed by the pursuit of knowledge he is highly adaptable and always eager to learn and help.

Having plenty of experience with 3d Studio Max, Photoshop, Davinci Resolve and the Unreal Engine, he is very versatile in all aspects of production and can create anything he sets his mind to.
